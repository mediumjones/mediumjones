Cody Jones

Cody is a visual and interaction designer based in Vancouver, BC. He is currently a freelancer practicing user interface design, user experience, branding and typeface design.

He recently graduated from Emily Carr University of Art + Design where he studied interaction design. During his studies he also worked as the Sr. Interaction Designer for Vancouver based software startup, EvidencePix.

Cody is also a skate­boarder, sandwich enthusiast, motorcycle lover, home-brewer and cartoon show buff.

You can usually find him hanging around Twitter, Github, and Rdio.
