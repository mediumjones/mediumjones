What is it?

Loverface is a new font that magically gives your writing a positive outlook. No longer will you have to worry about sounding like a grumpy old curmudgeon.

Loverface was created using Goudy Bookletter 1911 under SIL’s Open Font License.

How does it work?

Loverface uses ligatures to replace entire words in your writing. With a library of over 4,000 different negative terms, Loverface knows just what you’re really trying to say.
