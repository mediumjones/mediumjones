What I wish I knew when I left design school

Words of Wisdom is an opportunity for students and new designers to hear professionals in the industry share their stories and advice around the transition from design school and how they got to where they are now. Presentations are followed by an audience participatory design jam with the firms around how to better connect ECUAD Design students to industry in Vancouver.

Speakers

Steven Cox

Cause+Affect

Steven Cox is a founding principal and the Creative Director of Cause+Affect, a multi-disciplinary creative agency in vancouver.

Zoe Garred

Fleet Objects

Fleet Objects is the Vancouver based design studio of Zoe Garred. Working primarily with natural materials, Zoe's passion lies in creating objects that are useful but abstract in form and application.

Haig Armen

LiFT Studios

Haig is the Creative Director of LiFT Studios and has been creating strategies, designing and developing branding, advertising and interactive projects for over 12 years.

Nicholas Meyer

Nico Spacecraft

Nico Spacecraft is an award winning progressive 'design & build' studio involved in the creation of unique furnishings, kitchens, cabinetry, interiors and architectural elements.

Linus Lam & Denise Liu

Artsy Dartsy

With shared interest and curiosity in the power and mystery of design, Linus & Denise founded Edison & Sprinkles in 2008.

Tak Yukawa

Free Agency Creative is a contemporary graphic design and brand management firm specializing in creating design solutions for local and international companies.
